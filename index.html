RigFloorCalc

RigFloorCalc includes 11 most common calculations run during drilling operations in the field. Accurate and quick calculations while the operations are in progress save costly rig time, improve safety and prevent expensive errors.


This app was developed using the oil-field units with option to switch depth/length to meters where appopriate.

RigFloorCalcSI

RigFloorCalcSI is very similar to RigFloorCalc described here. The major difference is that System International units used for the input/output. Sure, alternate unit switch is omitted from this app.
